I don't really have that many achievements in life (or just don't like to boast about them). Well, I guess that's because I don't find the meaning of striving for status or material things. I mean, everything in life with soon come to an end and nothing really matters. What is the point of aiming to achieve the best or working so hard for man's empty praises.

There must be more to life than being the top of the class and to get a good job and to settle down with a family. I believe that our lives were created for a greater purpose. And finding that purpose will lead to a more fulfilling life.
