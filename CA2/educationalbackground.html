My Educational Background

I went to Jurong Primary School from the years 2007 to 2012. As we lived nearby, my parents decided to sign both of us into that school.

I graduated primary school with a PSLE aggregate of 188 and I decided to enroll into the normal academic stream in Fuhua Secondary School from the years 2013 to 2016.

I used to really love Physics when I was in Secondary school and was decent in the subject.

I scored 7 points for 'N' levels which enabled me to enter polytechnic through the Poly Foundation Program (PFP).

I chose the course Diploma in Information Technology (DIT) in Singapore Polytechnic (SP).

And so, that leads me to where I am now, a first year student in SP DIT.
